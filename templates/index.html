Our Story


Tippr was born of the frustration of calculating tips and track them by hand at an international drink chain. We knew there had to be a better way, and the Tippr platform was born of the labor of many sleepless nights.


Pricing

Tippr is Currently Free! However we plan to move to a model of charging $1/month per user, sadly, we gotta pay the bills. We could also switch to a patreon model, depending on support.
